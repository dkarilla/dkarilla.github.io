Brazilian Recipes

Rice and beans is a staple in the Brazilian diet. Brazilians are also meat-eaters. Feijoada is a dish the combines the two. Usually with black beans, feijoada follows the basic steps of cooking black beans, but with various meat parts and sausages boiled with the beans. In this video, my mother describes when feijoada is and the basic process of cooking it.
