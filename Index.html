About Karilla Dyer

Karilla Dyer is a third-year public relations major at the University of Florida College of Journalism and Communications. She is an enthusiastic, hard working young woman who excels in social media management, research and planning. Her strengths are her responsibility, integrity and positivity.

Karilla is a dual citizen of Brazil and was raised to be a world traveler. She has a passion for learning about other cultures and is an advocate for diversity. Karilla has a love of teaching and mentoring. She has helped guide freshman first-generation students through college as a First Year Florida peer leader. She has taught a listening and speaking course for students learning English at the University of Florida English Language Institute. Karilla blogs about film and writes fiction stories in her free time.
